🎯 Career Coach

AI-Powered Job Application Assistant

Your personal guide to landing your dream job

Career Coach is an intelligent job application platform that helps you craft compelling resumes, cover letters, and interview preparation materials tailored to your target positions.

Powered by AI, Career Coach analyzes job descriptions and your background to provide personalized recommendations and content generation.

Key Features

  • AI-powered resume optimization
  • Tailored cover letter generation
  • Job description analysis
  • Interview preparation guides
  • Career path recommendations
  • Application tracking
  • Document management
  • Real-time feedback and suggestions
